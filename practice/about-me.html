About

During my senior year of high school, I was asked to help an ESL first-grader with his transition to school. He didn't speak much English and didn't go to Kindergarten so he was having a lot of trouble adjusting. I worked with him during the week to help with translating instructions, explaining classroom rules, and guiding him. As the year progressed, he was able to understand more English and started to work more independently. I still continued to help in the classroom and got the opportunity to interact with other kids in the classroom as well. Overall, it was very special to be able to see his progress and I'm glad I was able to help him with his first grade year!
